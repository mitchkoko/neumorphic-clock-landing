Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 28, 2025

Neumorphic Clock is designed with your privacy in mind. As a simple clock widget app, we believe in complete transparency about our minimal approach to data handling.

Our Privacy Commitment

Neumorphic Clock operates entirely on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

What We Don't Collect

  • No personal information or user data
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No location data
  • No contact information
  • No device identifiers
  • No network requests or data transmission

How the App Works

Neumorphic Clock is a standalone widget application that:

  • Displays the current time using your device's system clock
  • Stores widget preferences locally on your iPhone
  • Functions completely offline without internet connectivity
  • Does not communicate with any external servers

iOS Permissions

The app requires minimal system permissions:

  • Widget Extension: To display clock widgets on your home screen
  • System Time: To show the current time accurately

Data Storage

All preferences and settings are stored locally on your device using iOS's standard storage mechanisms. This data never leaves your device and is automatically removed when you delete the app.

Third-Party Services

Neumorphic Clock does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics tools, or advertising networks.

Children's Privacy

Since we don't collect any data, Neumorphic Clock is suitable for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

Any updates to this privacy policy will be reflected in the app's update notes. The date at the top of this page indicates when the policy was last revised.

Your Rights

As we don't collect any personal data, there is no data to access, modify, or delete. You maintain complete control through your device's settings and can remove the app at any time.
